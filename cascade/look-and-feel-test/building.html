Geisel Library

Designed to look like hands lifting knowledge into the air, Geisel Library offers eight floors of silent and collaborative study spaces, over 1 million printed works, and librarians ready to help you track down exactly what you need.

7:30 AM - Midnight (Today's Hours)
9701 Hopkins Dr, La Jolla, CA 92093

About Geisel

Eight floors with every kind of study environment, Geisel is where you come to get things done. Open collaboration and group work on the lower levels, quieter individual study above, and a dedicated silent floor at the top for deep focus. Reservable study rooms, outlets throughout and campus Wi-Fi are available on every floor.

When you need a break, de-stress spaces are scattered throughout the building. When it’s time to tackle serious research and dive into our collection of over 7 million items, and staff are on hand to point you in the right direction.

Wat to explore resources beyond that? Check out the Teaching + Learning Commons, Special Collections & Archives and the Data & GIS Lab-- three spaces where you can get support beyond the stacks.

Geisel Tunnel

Running through the heart of Geisel's first floor and connecting the east and west wings, the tunnel is a warm, softly lit passage that doubles as one of the best spots on campus to study with friends or take a much-needed break in one of the specialized nap pods designed to block out noise and help you recharge.

Geisel Large Adaptive Study Room (GLASS)

Got a big group? Geisel GLASS seats up to 16 people, making it the perfect study room to reserve for large group projects.

Student Community Space

Student orgs, meet your new favorite meeting spot. This reservable Student Community Space is designed with groups in mind, comfortably seating approximately 8 to 10 people, a large conference table, and everything you need to plan, collaborate, and get things done.
