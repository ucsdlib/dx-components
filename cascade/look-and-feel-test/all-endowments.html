Library Endowments

Endowments empower the UC San Diego Library to enhance collections, improve student experiences, and maintain academic excellence. Search or browse our Library Endowments to learn more about the groundbreaking discoveries, treatments, and intellectual achievements they have generated.

    Supporting the UC San Diego Librarys efforts to enhance, preserve, and digitize their Melanesian collections for broader, long-term access.

  • NEH Challenge Grant Endowment
